Help and Frequently Asked Questions

How can I vote for my favorites?

To vote for your favorites, follow these steps:
  1. Search for the desired actor in our database.
  2. Click the "Add to favorites" button next to the actor's image.
  3. The actor will be added to your favorites list.

How can I log in or register?

To log in or register on the platform, follow these steps:
  1. Access the "MY ACCOUNT" page from the main menu.
  2. Fill out the form with the necessary information, such as email address and password (first name + last name if you don't have an account yet).
  3. Click the "Log in" or "Sign up" button to complete the process.
